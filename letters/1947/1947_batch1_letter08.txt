Note Left Under a Coffee Cup

Date: 1947-10-15
Recipient: Stranger with the Green Umbrella
Location: Café de Flore, Paris
Year: 1947
Themes: chance, city, flirtation, observation, smoke

you sat where the city keeps its second heartbeat
steam made a small cathedral over our cups and we were both believers for six sips
outside the boulevard rehearsed its applause for the evening

you read a newspaper until it turned soft in your hands; the rain edited the headlines politely
somewhere a radio rehearsed an accordion’s memory and the room exhaled smoke that curled like handwriting

i do not know your name, only the umbrella that translated October into green
only the way you looked at the window as if the glass had written you a letter first

if you find this note, return it to the saucer and pretend it has always lived there
a prophecy is only a napkin with careful crumbs
may your pockets discover two coins exactly when the night requires them

[This is a fictional creative work inspired by the style of E.E. Cummings and is not an authentic historical letter.]
