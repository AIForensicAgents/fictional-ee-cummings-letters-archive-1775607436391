Room with palms listening

Date: 1941-11-05
Recipient: Lina
Location: Honolulu, Territory of Hawaii
Year: 1941
Themes: tropical, anticipation, art, love, city, play

dear Lina,

the palms hold their green breath and gossip with the trade winds; even the shirts on the line learn to dance before they are dry. afternoons, a ukulele climbs the stairs of the air and sits on the roof to look important. i look up from my sketchbook and forget the names of the colors on purpose so they can introduce themselves again.

sailors move along the street like bits of brave weather stitched to white. in the market i bought a pineapple that refused to be serious and a bouquet that smelled like a poem not yet chosen. the ocean keeps unbuttoning the shore and buttoning it again, as if it cannot decide which dress is more becoming.

i keep a chair for you on the lanai. it is empty in a very articulate way.

from this blue window,

— h
